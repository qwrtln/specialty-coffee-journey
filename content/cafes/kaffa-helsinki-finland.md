The venue regularly hosts training sessions and workshops for coffee enthusiasts.
Unfortunately, none were scheduled during our visit.

What truly distinguishes this place is their exceptional hand-brewed coffee offered at remarkably reasonable prices.
The taste was so impressive that we decided to [buy a bag](/coffee/2024/kaffa-colombia-el-refugio) after the first sip.

Their dedication to coffee extends beyond the beverages - even the wall artwork is created using coffee as a medium.
These simple sketches provide the perfect backdrop for a relaxing coffee experience.
