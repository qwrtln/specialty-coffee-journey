Beyond the Arctic Circle in winter twilight, especially in a small town like Tromsø, you'd expect to be far from specialty coffee culture.
Yet this place emerges as a hidden gem, delivering exactly what coffee enthusiasts seek.

|![Riso](/cafes/riso-1.jpg)|
|:---:|
|*Captured moments before the morning rush.*|

At precisely 10 a.m., as soon as the host turned the key, customers flooded in and claimed most tables.
While our breakfast and Chemex-brewed coffee took some time to arrive, the cozy wooden interior - perfectly matched to the snowy landscape outside - made the wait pleasant.
What stood out was the sight of Chemex brewers on nearly every table - a rare and impressive commitment to pour-over coffee both by baristas and customers alike.

The impeccable breakfast was followed by our first encounter with Drop Coffee - a well-rounded brew that prompted us to [take a bag home](/coffee/2022/drop-coffee-kenya-kamwangi).
